About

Furry Fashions exists to outfit your animal pals with only the very best in pet fashion. You are up to date with the latest trends, so why should your animal buddies miss out? We only stock products from the brands that matter, so that your creature friends never have to worry about feeling underdressed at their next gala or political fundraiser. Our brands will stand up to your animals, whether they behave like an illustrious member of society, or an undistinguished beast. If your animals destroy our fashion, we’ll replace it for free! Even the most unfortunate of creatures can be fashionable, our yearly “Haberdashery on the Streets” charity event helps homeless animals get all dapper and keep up with the times.
